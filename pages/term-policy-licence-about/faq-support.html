Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping takes 2-3 business days, and Next Day Delivery is available for select locations. Please note that these timeframes do not include order processing time, which is usually 1-2 business days.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to select international destinations. Shipping rates and delivery times vary depending on the destination. Please contact our customer service for more information.

How do I care for my Cocomatik products?

Care instructions vary by product. Please refer to the care label or product description for specific care instructions. Generally, we recommend gentle handling and following the provided care guidelines to maintain the quality and longevity of your products.

Can I change or cancel my order?

You can request changes or cancellations within 2 hours of placing your order by contacting our customer service. Once an order has been processed, we cannot guarantee that changes or cancellations can be accommodated.

How can I contact customer service?

You can reach our customer service team via email at support@Cocomatik.com.
